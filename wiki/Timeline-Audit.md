# Timeline audit

`Timeline audit` opens the timeline audit modal.

Use it when you want a diagnostic pass over the manuscript timeline rather than a repair workflow.

## What It Checks

Timeline audit can surface:

*   contradictions
*   missing `When` values
*   summary/body disagreement
*   continuity problems
*   unresolved findings that need review

It shows overview stats, finding filters, and finding cards with evidence and suggested actions.

## Audit Modes

The audit includes a deterministic pass and can optionally run a continuity pass for deeper checks.

When available, AI-enhanced findings are shown in the same review surface, but the command is still centered on audit and review rather than bulk rewriting.

## What You Can Do

From the audit modal, you can:

*   filter findings by issue type
*   inspect evidence and chronology placement
*   mark items for review
*   apply accepted fixes where supported
*   rerun the audit after changes
