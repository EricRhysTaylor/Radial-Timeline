# Search timeline

`Search timeline` opens the timeline search bar.

Use it when you want to find scenes by text or metadata without leaving the radial view.

## What It Searches

Search checks case-insensitive text across multiple fields, including:

*   title
*   synopsis
*   characters
*   subplot
*   location
*   POV
*   duration

Search requires at least **3 characters**.
